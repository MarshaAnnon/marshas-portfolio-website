About Me

Hi, I'm Marsha, originally from the UK, now based in NYC. My journey into tech started when I had my daughter & like many women had a choice to make. I chose flexibility & decided to open my online shop Color Me WHIMSY. I took classes in Entrepreneurship, SEO & Social Media, designed & customized my store myself.

I loved the technical side of running my business. I grew passionate about learning new methods to stay up-to-date with new trends. I loved passing on my knowledge & helping others I met with their pain points. I decided to pivot, I found a great course to teach me the basics & some excellent mentors to guide my journey. Now here I am living, learning, & leveling up one day at a time.
