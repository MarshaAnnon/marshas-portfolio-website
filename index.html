About Me

Based in NYC, my journey into tech started when I had my daughter & like many women I had a choice to make. I decided to create my online store Color Me WHIMSY. I took classes in Entrepreneurship & designed my store myself, doing everything from customization to SEO to Social Media.

I loved the technical side of running my business & grew passionate about learning new methods to stay current & up-to-date with new trends. I loved passing on my knowledge & helping others with their pain point so I decided to take the leap. I found a great course to teach me the basics & some excellent mentors to guide my journey, now here I am living, learning, & leveling up one day at a time.
